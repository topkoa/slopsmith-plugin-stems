For multi-stem sloppak songs, per-stem buttons appear in the player controls so you can toggle individual stems (guitar, bass, drums, vocals, etc.) on and off during playback. Each button fills left to right with its saved volume; drag left or right on a button to adjust it. Mute and volume state is remembered per song. PSARC songs are unaffected.

Checked stems start silenced when a song opens. Per-song toggles still override.
